Why we publish illustrations, not client stories

Board-level governance work is confidential by design. The engagements that matter most, the contested decisions, the supervisory pressure, the exit that nearly came apart, are precisely the ones no board wants described in a consultancy's marketing. We think that confidentiality is part of the service, so we do not publish client work, named or anonymised.

Instead, the engagements below are illustrations. Each is a constructed scenario showing how a Marentis Labs engagement operates in practice: the situation a board typically faces, the way the methodology is applied, and the position the engagement is designed to leave the board in. The situations are drawn from patterns our principal has seen across twenty years of institutional risk and governance work. No detail describes or derives from any client of Marentis Labs.

If you want to test whether the approach fits your situation, the faster route is a conversation. Schedule a confidential call.
