## The Situation

## The Governance Challenge

What specifically was broken or at risk? This section should name the failure mode with precision:

- Was the board receiving poor quality information?
- Was risk appetite defined but not operationalised?
- Were committee structures creating accountability gaps?
- Was the organisation facing a regulatory deadline or enforcement risk?

This is where the adversarial framing comes in. Don't write "the board needed to improve." Write what was actually failing and why that mattered.
